### 🧠 Replit Project Prompt: “Research Project Management System”

**Goal:**
Build a web-based Project Management System that allows **faculty members/doctors** to submit research or project proposals. The projects are then reviewed and graded by **editors** and partially by an **AI evaluation module**.

---

### 🧩 System Overview:

#### 1. **Roles**

* **Faculty/Doctor (Submitter):**

  * Can log in and submit a new project proposal with a title, description, department, budget (in KD), and attached files.
  * Can view submission status, grading results (AI + editor grading), and assigned editors.

* **Editor:**

  * Can view assigned projects.
  * Reviews and grades each project (60% weight).
  * Adds comments and recommendations.

* **Process Lead (Admin):**

  * Has full access to logs, analytics, and AI evaluations.
  * Can assign/reassign projects to editors.
  * Can view grading breakdowns, averages, and audit trails.

* **AI Evaluation Module (Internal):**

  * Automatically evaluates each submission for:

    * Relevance to university mission and vision.
    * Alignment with research center goals.
    * Completeness and clarity.
  * Produces an internal score (40% weight).

---

### ⚙️ **Grading Logic**

1. **AI Grading (40%)**

   * Automatically triggered upon submission.
   * Uses a local API endpoint `/api/ai-grade` later i will be proveded it's gonna be a deepseek api with knowledge base to evaluate and return a score between 0–40. 

2. **Editor Grading (60%)**

   * Each assigned editor provides a score between 0–60.

3. **Editor Assignment Rules:**

   * **Budget ≤ 20 KD:** assign to **2 editors** → average their grades.
   * **Budget > 20 KD:** assign to **3 editors** → average their grades.

4. **Final Grade = (AI 40%) + (Average of Editor(s) 60%)**

5. **Once graded:**

   * The system notifies the project owner (faculty/doctor) with the total score and editor feedback.

---

### 🗂️ **Data Models (MongoDB or PostgreSQL)**

**User:**

* id, name, email, role (faculty, editor, process_lead), department

**Project:**

* id, title, description, budget, submitter_id, status, ai_score, editor_scores[], final_score, assigned_editors[], createdAt, updatedAt

**Logs:**

* id, project_id, user_id, action (submit, assign, grade, reassign), timestamp

---

### 🖥️ **Tech Stack**

* **Frontend:** React + TailwindCSS
* **Backend:** Node.js (Express.js)
* **Database:** MongoDB (Mongoose)
* **AI Evaluation (Mock initially):** Node route that simulates grading with a random value or a simple keyword analysis function.
* **Authentication:** JWT-based auth (faculty, editor, process lead).

---

### 🧩 **Core Features**

1. **Project Submission Form**

   * Faculty fills out and uploads proposal.
   * AI instantly evaluates and logs score.

2. **Automatic Editor Assignment**

   * Based on budget rules (2 or 3 editors).
   * Sends notification (email or dashboard alert).

3. **Grading Dashboard**

   * Editors view pending projects and submit grades.
   * System calculates final average automatically.

4. **Admin Dashboard**

   * Full visibility: project list, logs, AI results, assignments.
   * Ability to override or manually reassign editors.

5. **Notifications**

   * Faculty notified after grading completion.
   * Editors notified when new projects are assigned.
